Cookies Policy

Introduction

At BlueRoot Garden Irrigation, we value your privacy and aim to be transparent about the data we collect and use through cookies and similar technologies. This Cookies Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices regarding their use.

By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy unless you choose to disable them.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored by your web browser on your device when you visit websites. They help websites remember your preferences, recognize repeat visits, and provide enhanced functionality.

Cookies can be first-party (set by the website you are visiting) or third-party (set by external services used by the website).

Types of Cookies We Use

We utilize different categories of cookies on our website to ensure optimal performance and user experience:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the basic functioning of our website, such as security, page navigation, and access to secure areas. They cannot be disabled as they are crucial to deliver our services.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous information about usage and errors. This helps us improve functionality and content.
  • Functional Cookies: These cookies allow the website to remember choices you make (such as language or region) and provide enhanced, personalized features.
  • Advertising and Targeting Cookies: These cookies track your browsing activity to deliver personalized advertisements relevant to your interests. They may be set by us or our advertising partners.

How We Use Cookies

Cookies on our website help us:

  • Ensure security and proper functioning of services.
  • Analyze website traffic and user behavior to improve our offerings.
  • Personalize content and remember your preferences for a better experience.
  • Deliver relevant advertising when you visit third-party websites.
  • Provide social media features and integrations.

Third-Party Cookies

We may allow third-party service providers, such as analytics and advertising partners, to set cookies on our site for the purposes outlined above.

Managing and Disabling Cookies

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. The process varies depending on your browser but generally involves accessing preferences or privacy settings.

Please note that disabling certain cookies, especially essential ones, may impact the functionality and usability of our website.

For detailed instructions on managing cookies in popular browsers, please visit:

  • Google Chrome

Your Cookie Consent Choices

When you first visit our website, you may receive a prompt requesting your consent to use cookies. You can choose to accept or decline non-essential cookies.

Accepting cookies helps improve your experience and allows us to deliver personalized content and offers. You can withdraw or change your consent at any time via browser settings or our cookie preferences (if available).

Declining cookies may limit some features or content but will not prevent access to essential site functions.
